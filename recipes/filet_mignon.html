Filet Mignon

Description

A classic, classy dish that's hard to mess up. Perfect for when you want to feel fancy without actually putting forth an effort.

Ingredients

  • Filet mignon
  • Butter
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Pat the steaks dry and season them heavily with salt and pepper on both sides
  2. Add the butter to a cast iron pan and let it melt
  3. Put the steak into the pan and let it cook for 2 minutes on each side and then grab a pair of tongs and sear all of the edges of the steak
  4. Put the pan with the steak into the oven for 10 minutes
  5. Remove the pan with the steak and let it rest for at least 10 minutes before serving

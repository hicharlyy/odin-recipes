Keto Cheesy Garlic Bread

Description

Cheesy, garlicky bread with only a fraction of the carbs. Garlic, cheese, almond flour and egg come together to make this delicious treat that any bread-lover would enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Almond flour
  • Cream cheese
  • Garlic
  • Baking powder
  • 1 Egg
  • Butter
  • Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Add mozzarella cheese and cream cheese into a bowl and microwave until soft
  2. Mix mozzarella cheese and cream cheese together until it is well combined
  3. Add almond flour and baking powder to the bowl and stir until the ingredients are well combined
  4. Add egg and mix until you have a paste
  5. Mince garlic and set it to the side
  6. Put butter into a microwave-safe bowl and microwave it for 30 seconds or until it starts to melt
  7. Remove butter from the microwave, add the minced butter into the bowl and stir
  8. Take a spoon and drop a spoonful of your batter onto a lined baking tray
  9. Brush the garlic butter mixture on top of each spoonful of batter and top it with a sprinkle of cheddar cheese
  10. Bake for 30 minutes on 350 or until golden and firm
